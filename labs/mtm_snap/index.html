MTM Snap

will langford

cba


I built an MTM Snap to take to a workshop in India.



The guts of the machine are mostly the same as the original design. I did deisgn my own z-stage and spindle though. The spindle uses a brusless motor powered at 15v and spins at roughly 15.3k RPM. This is about 2x what the spindle on the Modela spins at and so allows you to cut boards faster (and more cleanly, I think).



The machine is controlled by a TinyG and uses nice Pololu bipolar steppers.


The machine also makes extensive use of yellow expandable sleeving.
